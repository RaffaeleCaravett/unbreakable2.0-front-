  • Sleep and Illnesses

    Chronic sleep deprivation is linked to an increased risk of various health issues, including cardiovascular diseases, diabetes, and obesity. Lack of sleep may compromise the body's ability to fight off illnesses.

  • Sleep and Immune System

    Sleep is vital for a robust immune system. Insufficient sleep can weaken the immune response, making the body more susceptible to infections and slowing down the recovery process.

    Lack of sleep can disrupt hormonal balance, affecting hormones related to stress, appetite, and growth. This imbalance may contribute to weight gain, increased stress levels, and a higher risk of metabolic disorders.
